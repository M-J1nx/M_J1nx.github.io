Enable 2FA and App Password for Gmail

If you encounter the error: 535-5.7.8 Username and Password not accepted., this could be due to Google removing the "less secure app" option as of May 30, 2022. To resolve this, follow the steps below:

Step 1: Enable 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)

Enable 2FA on the Google account you intend to use for sending emails. Follow the official guide here.

Step 2: Generate an App Password

After enabling 2FA, create an app password by accessing the following link:

https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords

Instructions:

  1. Enter the app name (you can choose any name you like).
  2. Once created, a password will be shown.

⚠️ Note: The password will only appear once when you create it, so make sure to save it securely.

Step 3: Use the App Password

Replace your Google account password with the app password in your email-sending service configuration.
